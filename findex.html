A unique speaking experience covering industry topics like:

Ken founded The Knowledge Coop in 2003 with one goal in mind: To create training that would change the way the mortgage industry did business.

Having spent time as a loan originator and accidentally committing fraud on his first day on the job, he knew there had to be a better way. Combining his passion for knowledge with his industry humor, Ken has forged a path in the industry to create edutainment that is changing how people think. Ken takes that passion on the road with speaking events all over the U.S.
